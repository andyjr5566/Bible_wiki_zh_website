# 來9：14

## 定義

基督藉著永遠的靈，將自己無瑕疵獻給神，BH以此節說明利未記22章無殘疾祭牲的要求預表基督毫無瑕疵的獻上。

## 按書卷累積

### 利未記

<!-- accumulation:利未記:22:start -->
#### [[03 利未記/第22章|第22章]]
- BH：It also foreshadows the ultimate offering of Jesus Christ, who presented Himself as a sacrifice for humanity's sins, as seen in Hebrews 9:14, which speaks of Christ offering Himself "without blemish to God." This can be seen as a type of Christ, who was the perfect and unblemished sacrifice (Hebrews 9:14).
